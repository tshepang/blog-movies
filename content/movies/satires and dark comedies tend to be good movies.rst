satires and dark comedies tend to be good movies
================================================

:date: 2014-03-13

list instead of two. Following is a list of very-good__ (or better)
movies that fit the description:

#. `Requiem for A Dream`__
#. Bamboozled
#. `Being John Malkovich`__
#. `Dr. Strangelove`__
