*Many satires tend to be (dark) comedies, and that's why I made one
list instead of two.*

- Bamboozled
- `Being John Malkovich`__
- `Dr. Strangelove`__
- Fight Club
- `Lord of War`__
- Payback__
- `Requiem for A Dream`__
