my movie rating system
======================

:tags: untagged


Here is what I mean by the rating tags:

* A **landmark** is any movie I would give 10/10;
  so far I don't have any, closest being `Fight Club`__.

* Following is the rest:

  * 9/10 `masterpiece`__
  * 8/10 `excellent`__
  * 7/10 `very good`__
  * 6/10 `good`__
  * 5/10 `decent`__
  * 4/10 `average`__
  * 3/10 `substandard`__
  * 2/10 `near-trash`__
